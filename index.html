Paramount architecture

Project Paramount is an ambitious, global initiative aimed at revolutionizing the way our company provides customer service and field service operations. At its core, the project leverages the robust capabilities of Microsoft Dynamics 365, incorporating the Customer Service and Field Service applications to create a seamless and efficient ecosystem. This system will empower Call Center Agents, Field Service Technicians, and Back-Office Users across all regions around the world, ensuring they can deliver exceptional service and support to our customers.

The architecture of Project Paramount integrates advanced technologies such as Azure Communication Services for phone calls and omnichannel services for chat, telephony, and email, ensuring versatile and reliable communication streams. Power BI will be utilized for comprehensive reporting, providing real-time insights and analytics to drive informed decision-making. Security and compliance are of utmost importance, with EntraID being used for authentication, and Microsoft Teams facilitating internal communications. Additionally, the project incorporates a D2C cross-channel sales capability to streamline product and payment integrations, alongside the utilization of Azure services like API Management, Logic Apps, and Event Grid to manage data exchange and workflows. By harnessing these technologies, Project Paramount aims to set a new standard in customer and field service excellence, enhancing operational efficiency and customer satisfaction on a global scale.

This diagram illustrates the integration of Microsoft Dynamics 365 applications with various Azure services, external systems, and communication channels, offering a clear overview of the end-to-end data flow and functionality across the global operation.
